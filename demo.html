Pachmarhi

Places to Visit in Pachmarhi

Bee Fall

This is a waterfall in Pachmarhi.
 It is named as such because from a distance the waterfall sounds like a bee.
  Bee Falls takes its name from bees since this place is famous for honey bees

Silver Fall

Also called Rajat Prapat/Big Fall.
   The falls comes 2800+ feet roaring down the gorge creating a silver streak that gives its name.
    The view of dense, serene, virgin forest and stream is simply mesmerizin

Afsara Vihar

This is a mountain stream that creates a natural water pool,
  cascades and waterfall in its course.
